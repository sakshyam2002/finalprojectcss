Momo,a tibetian , Nepali cultural food was first discovered in Tibet but got popular in Nepal as Newari people were already eating Momo since decades before Tibet invented Momos.

DISCOVER MOMO

Dumplings that are filled with veggies or meat of your choice; we call it keema, that is filled with traditional flavors and authentic aroma.

Pork Momo

Pork Momo is made with pork meat ,butter ,onions and garlic along with all the spices .It's the juciest Momo trending these days .

Jhol Momo

Jhol Momo differs from place to place but it is basically served with loads of sauce. We call it jhol that is made of nuts,tomatoes,sesame seeds,and herbs which makes your experience of eating Momo 10 Times better .
